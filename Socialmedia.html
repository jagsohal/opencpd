DIGTAL CASE STUDIES OF YOUNG PEOPLE'S USES OF SOCIAL MEDIA

These evidence-based case studies demonstrate the varying ways young people use social media and the impacts of this behaviour. 

For more information about this project click here:

These evidence-based case studies demonstrate the varying ways young people use social media and the impacts of this behaviour on their health and wellbeing. The case studies are based on evidence generated with 1346 young people in the UK who worked with us to communicate their experiences of social media. This work was supported by the Wellcome Trust [201601/Z/16/Z]. The case studies are relevant to adults who have a responsibility for young people’s health and wellbeing.

If you would like to use these videos in your professional organisation or would like more information about the project please contact Dr Victoria Goodyear
