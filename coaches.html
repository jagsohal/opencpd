Play

If play is viewed as a valuable part of development and not seen as time wasted then we will harness its true potential. When coaching young players, play should be seen as the time when they are encouraged to innovate and improvise. It should be seen as a time for fun and enjoyment and be included because of the progress and learning that is possible when players are highly intrinsically motivated and fully engaged in the activities.

We also want to produce coaches who are flexible, adaptable and creative so they can engage with play in such a way that benefits all our players.
